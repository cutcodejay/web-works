AMHA Goal

The Asian Malignant Hyperthermia Alliance (AMHA) is a non-official, non-profit organization dedicated to addressing the challenges of malignant hyperthermia (MH).

AMHA focuses on the newest trends in MH treatment, aiming to enhance early detection and prevention of this potentially fatal condition.

The alliance works on creating a comprehensive database for logging MH susceptibility (MHS) and constructing detailed family trees to track genetic predispositions.

By promoting advanced research and fostering collaboration among international experts, AMHA strives to significantly reduce the loss of life and property caused by MH. Through these efforts, AMHA seeks to improve patient outcomes and support the medical community in managing MH more effectively.
