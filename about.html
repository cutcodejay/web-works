關於我 About Me

個人簡介 / Personal Introduction

我是 Jay，來自台灣，擁有超過 20 年以上的前端網站網頁切版與設計經驗。 專精於 HTML、CSS 與 RWD 響應式網頁技術，並熱衷於提升 UI/UX 設計與使用者體驗。

在我的職涯中，曾參與多個專案從無到有的規劃與製作，具備良好的跨部門溝通與協作能力。 我相信好的設計不只美觀，更應有效且有溫度，因此我致力於打造順暢且友善的使用者介面。

I'm Jay from Taiwan, with over 20 years of experience in front-end web development and layout design. Skilled in HTML, CSS, and responsive web design (RWD), I'm passionate about improving UI/UX design and user experience.

Throughout my career, I've been involved in planning and executing various projects from scratch, with strong cross-functional communication and collaboration skills. I believe good design should not only look beautiful but also be functional and empathetic. I strive to create smooth and user-friendly interfaces.

工作經歷摘要 / Career Summary

我自 2005 年至今累積超過 20 年前端開發經驗，服務過新媒體、設計公司與品牌端，參與過各類專案頁、活動頁與政府形象網站的建置。

我擅長從規劃、執行到上線的完整開發流程，並注重細節、效能與 UI/UX 的兼顧，能成為團隊中穩定可靠的執行者。

ince 2005, I’ve accumulated over 20 years of front-end layout experience, working with digital media, design studios, and brand-side companies. I have contributed to various project pages, campaign microsites, and government image websites.

自傳 / Personal Statement

網頁設計已從興趣轉變為我專職的事業。二十餘年來，我不斷專研技術與設計，累積了豐富的實戰經驗，深刻了解市場需求與使用者行為。我秉持著持續學習的精神，以確保我的服務與時俱進，保持競爭力。

我深信，一個成功的網站是團隊合作的結晶，而網頁設計師在其中扮演著至關重要的溝通橋樑。我擅長整合前端開發、UI/UX 設計和客戶需求，確保專案從概念發想到最終上線的每個環節都能順利執行。

在我的服務中，我不僅專注於視覺設計與程式碼實現，更重視網站的標準性、相容性、流通性與使用者體驗。我能協助客戶處理網站優化與行銷策略，例如SEO、社群媒體整合等，確保網站能在激烈的網路環境中脫穎而出，有效觸及目標受眾。

儘管市場瞬息萬變，但我相信人類的創意與設計思維是機器難以取代的。憑藉多年的歷練、紮實的能力和強大的學習力，我能為您提供量身打造的專業網頁解決方案。

我期待能有機會與您合作，運用我的專業為您的品牌或事業打造一個完善且具競爭力的網站。

Web design has evolved from a passion into my full-time profession. For over two decades, I have continuously refined my technical skills and design sensibility, gaining extensive hands-on experience and a deep understanding of market needs and user behavior. I uphold a spirit of continuous learning to ensure my services remain current and competitive.

I firmly believe that a successful website is the result of teamwork, and the web designer plays a key role as a communication bridge between parties. I excel at integrating front-end development, UI/UX design, and client requirements to ensure every phase—from concept to launch—runs smoothly.

My services go beyond visual design and coding—I focus on web standards, compatibility, accessibility, and user experience. I help clients with website optimization and marketing strategies—such as SEO and social media integration—to ensure their sites stand out and reach target audiences effectively.

Despite rapid market changes, I believe human creativity and design thinking remain irreplaceable by machines. With years of experience, solid skills, and strong learning ability, I can deliver custom professional web solutions tailored to your needs.

I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate and use my expertise to build a complete and competitive website for your brand or business.
