About Me

Personal Introduction

Hi, I'm Jay — Senior Visual Web Design × AI Application. 20 years of web visual design experience, crafting professional website visuals for businesses and brands.

I'm Jay, from Taiwan, with approximately 20 years of Internet design experience, specializing in front-end website layout, web design, and RWD responsive web development, with strong proficiency in HTML and CSS. My expertise also covers newsletters and EDMs, banner design and production, as well as website maintenance and management. I excel at translating user needs into intuitive, user-friendly interfaces, continuously advancing my UI/UX design and front-end skills to ensure every project achieves the highest standards of aesthetics, stability, and usability.

Throughout my career, I have participated in planning and producing numerous projects from scratch, covering websites, newsletters, EDMs, banners, and other digital design items, with strong cross-departmental communication and collaboration skills. I believe good design should not only look great but also be efficient and thoughtful — so I'm committed to creating intuitive, fluid, and user-friendly interfaces that meet high standards of aesthetics, stability, and usability.

🎨 Design Thinking in the Age of AI

AI tools are making website development faster than ever — but ensuring it looks great, feels professional, and aligns with the brand still requires an experienced designer's judgment.

Drawing on 20 years of visual design expertise, I plan with AI, operate AI, and guide AI to produce design directions that meet real requirements — then I conduct quality review and detail refinement. The result: higher efficiency, more precise outcomes. What you get isn't just a website, but a brand presentation backed by design logic.

Vibe Design ≠ The end of professional design. A designer with 20 years of visual judgment × AI-powered acceleration — that's the truly competitive combination.
