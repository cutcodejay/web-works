About Me

Personal Introduction

I'm Jay from Taiwan, with over 20 years of experience in front-end web layout and design. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and responsive web design (RWD), and I am passionate about enhancing UI/UX design and the overall user experience.

Throughout my career, I have participated in numerous projects from concept to completion, demonstrating strong cross-department communication and collaboration skills. I believe that great design is not only visually appealing but also functional and human-centered, which is why I strive to create smooth and user-friendly interfaces.

Career Summary

Since 2005, I’ve accumulated over 20 years of front-end layout experience, working with digital media, design studios, and brand-side companies. I have contributed to various project pages, campaign microsites, and government image websites.

I specialize in end-to-end project development—from planning to deployment—with attention to detail, performance, and UI/UX. I’m a dependable and consistent executor in any team.

Personal Statement

Web design has evolved from a passion into my full-time profession. For over two decades, I have continuously refined my technical skills and design sensibility, gaining extensive hands-on experience and a deep understanding of market needs and user behavior. I uphold a spirit of continuous learning to ensure my services remain current and competitive.

I firmly believe that a successful website is the result of teamwork, and the web designer plays a key role as a communication bridge between parties. I excel at integrating front-end development, UI/UX design, and client requirements to ensure every phase—from concept to launch—runs smoothly.

My services go beyond visual design and coding—I focus on web standards, compatibility, accessibility, and user experience. I help clients with website optimization and marketing strategies—such as SEO and social media integration—to ensure their sites stand out and reach target audiences effectively.

Despite rapid market changes, I believe human creativity and design thinking remain irreplaceable by machines. With years of experience, solid skills, and strong learning ability, I can deliver custom professional web solutions tailored to your needs.

I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate and use my expertise to build a complete and competitive website for your brand or business.
