About Me

Personal Introduction

I am Jay from Taiwan, with approximately 20 years of experience in Internet design. I specialize in front-end web development, website design, and RWD (responsive web design), with strong proficiency in HTML and CSS. My expertise also includes newsletters and EDMs, banner design and production, as well as website maintenance and management. I excel at translating user requirements into intuitive and user-friendly interfaces, while continuously enhancing UI/UX design and front-end skills to ensure that my work achieves the highest standards of aesthetics, stability, and usability.

Throughout my career, I have participated in numerous projects from concept to completion, covering websites, newsletters, EDMs, banners, and other digital design initiatives. I possess strong cross-department communication and collaboration skills. I believe that good design is not only visually appealing but also effective and thoughtful. Therefore, I am committed to creating intuitive, smooth, and user-friendly interfaces, ensuring that every project meets high standards of aesthetics, stability, and usability.

Personal Statement

For me, design started as a personal interest and gradually evolved into my full-time profession. Over approximately 20 years, I have continuously honed my skills in Internet-related technologies and digital projects, including websites, newsletters, EDMs, and banners, gaining extensive hands-on experience and a deep understanding of market needs and user behavior. I maintain a spirit of continuous learning to ensure my services and skills stay up-to-date and professionally competitive.

I firmly believe that a successful website is the result of team collaboration, with web designers playing a key role as communication bridges. I excel at integrating front-end development, UI/UX design, and client requirements, ensuring that every stage of a project—from concept to final launch—proceeds smoothly.

In my work, I focus not only on visual design and code implementation but also on standards compliance, compatibility, usability, and overall user experience. I assist clients with website optimization and marketing strategies, such as SEO and social media integration, to ensure projects stand out in a competitive digital environment and effectively reach their target audience.

Even in a rapidly changing market, I believe creativity and design thinking remain irreplaceable human advantages. With years of experience, solid skills, and a strong drive for continuous learning, I provide tailored professional web design solutions.

I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate with you, leveraging my expertise to create complete, high-quality, and competitive digital projects for your brand or business.
